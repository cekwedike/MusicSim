### 3. **Mental Health & Wellbeing for Artists**

**Category**: Wellness
**Difficulty**: Beginner
**Duration**: 8 minutes
**Unlock**: Career Progress 25%+

#### What It Teaches:

- **African Hustle Culture Trap**: How "sleep is for the weak" destroys artists
- **Family Pressure & Mental Load**: Managing financial expectations and cultural guilt
- **Public Scrutiny & Social Media**: Dealing with brutal African social media
- **Burnout Warning Signs**: Recognizing when you need to slow down
- **Practical Mental Health Strategies**: Therapy, boundaries, scheduled rest
- **When to Take a Break**: Strategic breaks vs. crisis-forced breaks

#### Real-World Examples:
- Kendrick Lamar's multi-year strategic breaks
- Kid Cudi and Billie Eilish taking mental health time off
- Nigerian mental health platforms (MindBody, WellNewMe)
- Artists collapsing from exhaustion during tours

#### Key Lessons:
- Hustle culture is destroying African artists - rest is not weakness
- Family financial pressure creates unique mental health challenges
- Burnout warning signs should be addressed immediately
- Strategic breaks save careers more than grinding through
- Therapy works even if culturally uncommon

**Category**: Collaboration

**Explanation:** "Cultural exchange involves mutual respect, proper credit, and fair compensation. Appropriation takes cultural elements without permission, credit, or compensation while often misrepresenting or erasing the source culture."
