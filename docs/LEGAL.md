We believe in **data minimization**‚Äîwe only collect what's necessary to provide you with a meaningful learning experience and improve the platform.

## 5. How We Protect Your Data

We take data security seriously and implement multiple layers of protection.

### 5.1 Encryption

**Data in Transit:**
- All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses **TLS 1.3 encryption**
- This means nobody can intercept and read your information as it travels across the internet

**Data at Rest:**
- User data is stored in **Supabase's PostgreSQL database** with **AES-256 encryption at rest**
- This protects your data even if someone somehow accessed our servers

### 5.2 Authentication Security

We use **Google OAuth** for authentication, which means:
- ‚úÖ You sign in with your existing Google account (secure and convenient)
- ‚úÖ We receive **only** your email address and basic profile information from Google
- ‚úÖ We **don't access** your Gmail, Drive, or other Google services
- ‚úÖ You benefit from Google's robust security infrastructure

### 5.3 Data Architecture Security

Here's something important about our architecture:
- **Identification data** (name, email) is stored **separately** from **learning progress data**
- We use **tokenized user IDs** to connect these databases
- This means your learning data can't easily be traced back to your identity, even if one database was compromised

### 5.4 Access Controls

- **Only essential personnel** can access personal data
- All data access is **logged and monitored**
- Administrative access requires **multi-factor authentication**
- We conduct **regular security audits** and vulnerability assessments

### 5.5 Breach Notification

If a security breach occurs, we will:
1. **Notify you within 72 hours** via email
2. **Inform relevant authorities** (NITDA in Nigeria, RISA in Rwanda)
3. **Explain what data was affected** and what steps we're taking
4. **Provide guidance** on protecting yourself

Transparency during incidents matters as much as prevention.
