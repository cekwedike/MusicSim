## What is MusicSim?

MusicSim is a **free educational game** developed by **Chidiebere Shedrack Ekwedike** as part of a BSc Software Engineering capstone project at **African Leadership University, Rwanda**.

It teaches **independent artists in Nigeria and Rwanda** about the music business through interactive scenarios and simulations. You learn by making decisions as an artist and seeing the outcomes.

## How We Protect Your Data

### Security Measures:
- **TLS 1.3 encryption** for data in transit
- **AES-256 encryption** for data at rest (Supabase PostgreSQL)
- **Google OAuth** for secure sign-in
- **Separate databases** for identity vs. learning data
- **Limited access** with multi-factor authentication
- **Regular security audits**

### If a Breach Occurs:
- We'll notify you within **72 hours**
- Inform authorities (NITDA in Nigeria, RISA in Rwanda)
- Explain what happened and what we're doing
- Provide guidance on protecting yourself
