iOS Splash Screen Converter

To convert SVG splash screens to PNG format:

  1. Use an online tool like CloudConvert
  2. Or use ImageMagick: convert splash.svg splash.png
  3. Or use an online PWA asset generator like PWA Builder

Recommended: Use PWA Asset Generator
